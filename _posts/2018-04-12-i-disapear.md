I understand nothing<br/>
I know nothing 

My mind shrinks<br/>
My insignificance grows

The remaining of me rises<br/>
Like a high mountain<br/>
into the dark sky.<br/>
	
My love crumbles:<br/>
Stones rolling;<br/>
Its surface touching<br/>
part of souls I will never live.

My mind shrinks<br/>
My love flies:

The window is open.
